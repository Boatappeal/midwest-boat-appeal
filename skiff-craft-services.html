Service and Restoration

Every Skiff Craft deserves a craftsman.

Full-service care for classic wooden boats — from a season-ready detail to a complete hull restoration. Performed at Midwest Boat Appeal and Marine Plywood, St. Bonifacius, Minnesota.

Hull Restoration

Ground-up rebuilds and structural restorations for Skiff Craft and classic wooden hulls.

▾
  • Full hull teardown and survey
  • Frame and rib replacement
  • Replanking and bottom replacement
  • Transom rebuild
  • Lapstrake plank repair and replacement
  • Deck replacement and recaulking
  • Structural fiberglass reinforcement

Varnish and Brightwork

Strip, sand, stain and varnish — mahogany topsides back to a mirror finish. 12–15 coats of marine varnish.

▾
  • Full strip and bare-wood preparation
  • Grain raising and stain matching
  • 12–15 coat marine varnish system
  • Hand-rubbed between coats
  • Interlux and Epifanes varnish
  • Annual brightwork maintenance coats
  • Teak brightwork and oiling

Paint

Topsides, hulls, bottoms and cabin soles. Anti-fouling bottom paint, boot stripe work, and custom color matching.

▾
  • Topside paint (Interlux, Epifanes, Awlgrip)
  • Anti-fouling bottom paint
  • Boot stripe painting and renewal
  • Cabin sole and interior paint
  • Custom color matching
  • Fairing and prep work
  • Clear coat and sealers
